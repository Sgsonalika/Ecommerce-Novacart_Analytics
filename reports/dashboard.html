🔴
31–50% Discount = Negative Margins
Orders at the deepest discount tiers generate NEGATIVE average profit margins. NovaCart is paying customers to buy. Immediate policy cap needed.
🔄
Discounts Drive Returns
Return rate rises sharply above 20% discount — deal-hunters treat purchases as "try before you buy." Returns cost ~₹10L annually in reverse logistics.
✅
Recommendation: Cap at 20%
Estimated ₹15–20L annual profit recovery from capping discounts at 20% max. Replace deep discounts with loyalty rewards and product bundles.

Strategic Recommendations — Prioritised by ROI
01 · HIGH PRIORITY
🔒 Discount Governance Framework
Cap all discounts at 20%. Require Category Manager approval for 16–20%, CFO + Category Head for any exception above. Replace deep discounts with loyalty cashback and product bundles.
Expected: +₹15–20L annual profit recovery
02 · HIGH PRIORITY
⭐ NovaCart Plus Loyalty Programme
Launch Silver (2–5 orders: free shipping + 5% cashback) and Gold (6–15 orders: express + 10% cashback + early access) tiers. Target conversion of 10% one-time buyers.
Expected: +₹6–8L revenue in 12 months
03 · MEDIUM PRIORITY
📍 East Region Expansion Pilot
Partner with a regional 3PL provider in Kolkata. Run a 90-day awareness campaign in Bhubaneswar and Guwahati. Measure conversion rate and NPS vs national benchmark.
Expected: +15% East revenue in 12 months
04 · HIGH PRIORITY
💼 B2B Corporate Channel
Assign 3 Key Account Managers for corporate clients. Launch bulk-order pricing catalogue for Technology and Office Supplies. Integrate GST invoice automation.
Expected: +25–35% Corporate revenue
05 · MEDIUM PRIORITY
👗 Category Mix Rebalancing
Increase Clothing SKU catalogue by 40 items. Reduce Technology markdown budget by 30%. Launch Home & Kitchen bundle packs. Blended margin improvement from 20.3% to 24–26%.
Expected: +4–6pp blended margin
06 · MEDIUM PRIORITY
📦 Return Rate Reduction
Mandatory 360° product images for Technology. "7-Day Try" programme for Clothing (Gold loyalty members). Smart discount gate: flag orders >25% discount for return risk review.
Expected: Return rate 19.8% → 14–15%
